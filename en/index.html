About Same-Age Masterpieces

Same-Age Masterpieces is a free tool: just enter your date of birth, and see the great works that famous writers created at the very age you are now. Discover what masters like Dostoevsky, Hemingway and the Brontë sisters wrote, and at what age — with a short summary of each.

Age is calculated as "year of publication − author's birth year," so there may be small inaccuracies (publication is not the same as writing).

Data is drawn automatically from the open database Wikidata and Wikipedia.

Source: Wikidata (free open database) / Age is estimated from publication year and the author's birth year.
